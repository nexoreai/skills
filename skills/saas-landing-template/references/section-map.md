# Section Map

## Page Order

1) Nav (fixed pill)
2) Hero
3) Social proof (logos)
4) Device showcase
5) Project management benefits
6) Financial management
7) Feature grid + secondary features
8) Testimonials
9) Pricing
10) Blog/insights
11) Community
12) CTA
13) Footer

## Key Layout Notes

- **Nav**: fixed pill with logo mark on the left, inline links (desktop), CTA button, and hamburger on mobile.
- **Hero**: centered copy stack + dual CTAs, followed by a large framed card containing skeleton UI blocks.
- **Logos**: single-line copy with a row of circular logo placeholders.
- **Device showcase**: large card with a web placeholder and a floating mobile placeholder in the bottom-right.
- **Benefits/Finance**: two-column copy + right/left skeleton card; tags render as pill chips.
- **Features**: three cards (customization, integrations grid, collaboration card) plus two secondary copy cards.
- **Testimonials**: left highlight card + right stacked quotes.
- **Pricing**: 3-column pricing cards, middle option highlighted with ring.
- **Blog**: featured card on left, two stacked cards on right.
- **Community**: two cards, each with short text and a CTA button.
- **CTA**: full-width card with headline and two buttons.
- **Footer**: three-column links + bottom legal strip.

## Accessibility

- Use `h1` for hero, `h2` for section titles.
- Buttons and links must be semantic elements (no divs for CTAs).
- Keep contrast at least WCAG AA for text against white/near-white surfaces.
